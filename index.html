Flash Cards

Time for A Quiz!

About

Flash Cards lets you easily create and manage stacks of flash cards. You can then flip through your stack in order to prepare yourself for your next quiz, test, or presentation. Tag your cards with chapters or subjects to keep everything organized.

Features

Card Stacks

Create stacks of flash cards that can be used to help you study for a test or learn a new topic.

Tagging

Tag cards within a stack to organize by chapter or subject.

Import/Export

Import or export a stack of cards from your Google Drive account!
