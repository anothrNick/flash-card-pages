Flash Cards Privacy Policy

This privacy policy discloses the privacy practices for Flash Cardsr (https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=io.sjostrom.flashcards).

Google Analytics

Flash Cards may collect information including your device type, operating system, network type, non-specific location, and language for analytical purposes. Please see the Google Analytics Privacy Policy - http://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/privacy/

AdMob

Flash Cards may report cookie and usage data to AdMob to display ads relevant to you. Please see Admob Privacy Policy - https://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/privacy/

Firebase

Flash Cards may report errors and crashes which occur during application usage via Firebase. Firebase is a cloud services provider and backend as a service company based in San Francisco, California. The company makes a number of products for software developers building mobile or web applications. Please see the Firebase Privacy Policy - https://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/privacy/

Security

All communications of data between the Flash Cards application and 3rd party services (as mentioned above) is performed over a secure network connection (HTTPS).

Consent

By using Flash Cards you consent to this privacy policy as well as all 3rd party privacy policies listed above.
